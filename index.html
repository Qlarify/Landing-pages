The four stages of every patient journey

Four moments. Four kinds of video. Every specialty.

Every library we build — cardiology, oncology, ortho, IVF, or any other specialty — is organised around the same four stages that every patient actually goes through.

01
Symptom Awareness

A patient notices a symptom and turns to YouTube, Google, Practo, and WhatsApp before calling a hospital.

What helps: Symptom explainers, red-flag guides, basic condition education.
02
Trust Building

The patient has decided they need a consult. Now they're choosing where and with whom.

What helps: Senior-specialist introductions, HOD profiles, clinical credentials.
03
Decision

The patient is weighing procedures, treatment paths, or specialist choice.

What helps: Procedure-comparison videos, decision aids, consented patient journey stories.
04
Post-Op Care

The patient has had treatment. Managing recovery. Future patients also watch this content.

What helps: Recovery-arc videos, rehab programmes, lifestyle guidance, outcome stories.
